Why This Website

We lived 100 years and raised two children between the two of us. By experience and constantly researching reliable information, we know a thing or two about what helps to live a healthier, simpler, and happier life. We want to share “a thing or two” with you. If you find it beneficial to your life, we will be genuinely delighted.

At the same time, this is a business endeavour, as we want to work from home and spend more time together as a family. First of all, we write articles of information that helps with daily life, including dressing, eating, playing, raising kids, and even fresher air and cleaner water.

In today's consumer world, most of us reply on purchasing what we need from third-party businesses, increasingly from e-commerce sites. Our articles focus on seeking natural materials in everyday purchases. Natural fibers are the best to human health, particularly to the developing, more vulnerable children and youth. They are also the friendliest to the environment. Synthetic materials, such as polyester and plastic, emit minute fibers everywhere, which could be breathed in or eaten, willingly or not. What is the best on the market to fulfill our needs? We believe that the guiding principles include the following:

Safety Benefit for health and growth
Comfort Value for money
Beauty and style

Much better at fulfilling such principles, particularly for children, are products that are 100% cotton, 100% organic cotton, 100% wool, all wood, all natural and organic ingredients, and contain no toxic color, paint, or element. Our motto is: Natural is best.

In the second step of our business, we want to turn some of our DIY projects into hand-made products, using all natural materials of the best quality that we can source. The third step is to build our own supply chain and create a brand or two. Well, this is a new and passionate journey for us. When you visit us on this website, we welcome you to watch and support our journey. We will smile if you take away "a thing or two".
