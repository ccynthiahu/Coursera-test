About Us

We care about what materials things are made from. No doubt natural materials are the best. They come from nature and return to nature. Natural fibers are the best to human health, particularly to the developing, more vulnerable children and youth. Synthetic materials, such as polyester and plastic, are epidemic in our lives. The minute fibers from such materials are everywhere and could be breathed in or eaten by us, willingly or not.

Sadly it is not easy to find products that are 100% cotton, 100% organic cotton, 100% wool, all wood, all natural and organic ingredients, and contain no toxic color, paint, or element. We tirelessly search for such products and want to share our findings with you. We also welcome you sharing your findings with us by the contact feature below. Together we can all live more natural, more organic, and healthier lives.

Our motto is: Natural is best.
