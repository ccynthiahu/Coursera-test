关于我们

We care about what materials things are made from. No doubt natural materials are the best. They come from nature and return to nature. Natural fibers are the best to human health, particularly to the developing, more vulnerable children and youth. Synthetic materials, such as polyeser and plastic, are epidemic in our lives. The minute fibers from such materials are everywhere and could be breathed in or eaten by us, willingly or not. A polyester and plastic environment is detrimental to the cognitive and physical development of children.

Sadly it is not that easy to find products that are "100% cotton", "100% organic cotton", "100% wool", all wood, all natural and organic ingredients, and contain no toxic color, paint, or element. This is why we tirelessly look for such products for ourselves and for our children. We want to share our findings with you to make your search easier. Together we can all live more natural, more organic, and healthier lives.

Our motto is: Natural is best.
